Our name

Everything has to start somewhere.

A cradle is where things begin — the origin point, the place that nurtures something into being. That is how we think about the work: we build the digital foundations a business is raised on, from the ground up.

The name also carries home with it. South Africa is the Cradle of Humankind, and we are proudly built here in Gauteng — local in origin, global in ambition.
